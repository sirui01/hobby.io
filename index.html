About myself

Hello! I'm Sirui, a current Year 2 NIE student. My teaching subjects are Biology and Chemistry. I own 2 cats, Rum and Raisin. They were adopted from the Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA) center back in 2019. Since then, I love to spend my free time taking care of them and playing with them!

More about my cats

Raisin

Being the older sibling of Rum, she upholds the responsibility of taking care of her younger brother. Compared to Rum, her fur is majority white with a few black spots in the center.

Rum (Rummie)

As with all younger siblings, Rum (Or as I like to call him, Rummie), is more mischievous. His fur is a more distinguished mix of both black and white colours, compared to his older sister.
